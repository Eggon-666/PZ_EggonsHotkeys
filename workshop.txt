title=Eggon's Hotkeys
description=This mod introduces hotkeys for actions on moveables (furniture) - pick up, place, dismantle & rotate and now also for SMOKIN'! No more excessive clicking!
description=
description=[b]You can choose the key bindings in the game's hotkeys menu![/b] Thanks to @ThereforeAliens for the know-how, and to @NoctisFalco for previous customization idea. Default bindings are the following:
description=
description=Pick up - press: ARROW UP
description=Place - press: ARROW DOWN
description=Dismantle - press: DELETE
description=Rotate - press: ARROW RIGHT
description=Smoke - press: C
description=
description=The smoking shortcut works even if items are in a bag and it packs them again to the relevant bags after smoking is finished!
description=
description=I play on build 41, don't know if it works on earlier builds. It might. If you tried and know if it works or not, please let me know.
